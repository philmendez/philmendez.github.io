My Inspiration

I was tired of building websites on Squarespace, Weebly, Wordpress, Wix, and Tumblr and wanted an extra challenge. I was curious how my friends, Jamie Cohen and Matt O'Hagan, built webistes from scratch and seemed to possess magical software engineering powers. I was teaching block-code to elementary school students.

All this coalesced and, when I had two free weeks over winter vacation, I got to work.
